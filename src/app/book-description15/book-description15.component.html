The Last Crypt

Buried under a reef off the Honduran coast, diver Ulises Vidal finds a 14th century bronze bell of Templar origin, sunk there more than a century before Christopher Columbus' discovery of America. Spurred on by curiosity and a thirst for adventure, together with a medieval historian and a daring Mexican archaeologist, he embarks on a search for the mythical treasure of the Order of the Temple in Spain, the Mali desert, the depths of the Caribbean and the Mexican jungle, facing a myriad of enigmas and dangers.
