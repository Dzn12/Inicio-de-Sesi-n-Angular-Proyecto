About Us

We are the NovelProject team

According to a report by the National Literacy in the USA, there have been notable changes in children’s enthusiasm for reading in the last few years. For example, less than 3 in 10 children and young people aged 8 to 18 read daily in 2023, this is a notable decrease compared with previous decades. The report claims that the decline in time spent reading will have negative consequences for children’s learning and life-chances.

NovelProject, our online reading platform, hopes to be a website where all readers can find interesting classic books to read for free. At the same time, in the future, we want to offer new authors the chance to reach readers and readers the chance to get to know new works alongside the classics. Readers attracted by the opportunity to read out-of-copyright classics on our vibrant, innovative, user-friendly platform, will be introduced to new original and fanfiction works presented alongside the old classics in a way that no other online reading and publishing platform has done.
