The Deadly Cavern Quest Hasers

During a high school assignment, Eevie and Tommy hear a "true" story they can't forget. While the entire class laughed at their friend Drew for his story of an evil tree that tried to kill him, the two friends can't help but listen carefully. When Eevie and Tommy set out to investigate the mysterious tree, they soon find themselves on their own exciting and potentially deadly adventure .... Sent into a magical realm filled with deadly traps, the two friends must solve a series of challenges, riddles and mysteries. But each escape only brings them closer to their doom. Eevie and Tommy will need to rely on their creativity...and each other to survive.
