Wars of the Roses: Stormbird: King Henry V - the great Lion of England - is long dead.In 1437 his kindly son comes of age and takes the throne. Frail in body and mind, King Henry VI relies on his supporters to run his kingdom. Richard, Duke of York, however, believes that without a strong king England will fall. His fears seem justified as English power is threatened by France, and discontent and rebellion spread at home.
