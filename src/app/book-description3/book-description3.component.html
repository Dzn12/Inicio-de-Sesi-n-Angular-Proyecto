Innovation City: A Portal Cultivation Fantasy Saga

Chaos looms over the beloved realm of Zemia. As Darkness and Light confront their greatest challenge, the Chosen – Aiden, Anthony, and Julia – wield newfound powers to combat the encroaching forces. With a blend of magic, cultivation, and strategic brilliance, the trio must revolutionize armor designs and unlock ancient enchantments..... In a world teetering on the brink, 'Innovation City' unfolds a tale of sacrifice, redemption, and the relentless pursuit of salvation. Will Aiden's barony on the edge of the Great Western Forest be enough to stem the tide, or will Chaos consume all?
