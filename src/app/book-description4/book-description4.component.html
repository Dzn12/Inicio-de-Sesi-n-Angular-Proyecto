Innovation City: A Portal Cultivation Fantasy Saga

Settle down, bake things and enjoy life. However, the gods ruined that dream. With [Baker] and a rare black ability, he is now on the run for his life, trying to become strong enough to return home. Now Max must find a way to make himself strong enough to survive the people and the god who is chasing him. Max, unprepared for the life of an adventurer, will have to learn things the hard way while hiding his secret so his companions won't turn on him.
