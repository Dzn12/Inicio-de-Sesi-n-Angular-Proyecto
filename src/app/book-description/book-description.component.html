The World Under The Mountain

A young man named Alex, who immerses himself in the virtual world of the game for the first time. With a feeling of excitement and nervousness, Alex finds himself in a digitally created landscape, marveling at the immensity and beauty of the world around him. Soon, he encounters other players and begins his journey in this new universe full of mystery and adventure. As he explores his surroundings, he faces the game's initial challenges and begins to discover the unique rules and dynamics of this virtual world.
